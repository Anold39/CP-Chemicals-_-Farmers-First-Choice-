Sustainability February 2026

New Eco-Friendly Packaging

At CP Chemicals, we believe that modern agriculture must go hand-in-hand with environmental stewardship. This year, we are proud to announce our transition to eco-friendly, recyclable packaging for our liquid chemical range.

This initiative aims to reduce plastic waste in rural farming communities by over 40% by the end of 2027. We are also introducing a 'Return-to-Branch' program where farmers can return empty, triple-rinsed containers for safe disposal and reward points toward their next purchase.

Protecting the Zimbabwean soil is our collective responsibility.

Preparing for the Winter Wheat Season

The 2026 Winter Wheat season is fast approaching. Proper preparation in March is the difference between a record harvest and a struggling crop. Our agronomists have identified the five critical areas for success.

  1. Soil Testing: Ensure your pH levels are optimized for wheat.
  2. Seed Selection: Use drought-resistant and high-yield varieties like SC-Wheat Series.
  3. Calibration: Check your sprayers now to avoid uneven chemical application.
  4. Early Fertilization: Nitrogen management is key in the first 21 days of emergence.
  5. Pest Scouting: Be on high alert for rust and aphids in the early stages.

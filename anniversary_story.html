Announcement April 2026

Celebrating 20 Years of Growth

In April 2006, CP Chemicals opened its doors with a simple mission: to provide Zimbabwean farmers with high-quality, effective agrochemicals. Today, as we celebrate 20 years of service, that mission remains unchanged.

From our first small branch to a nationwide network, we have grown alongside the farmers who feed our nation. Our Managing Director noted, "The success of CP Chemicals is mirrored in the success of the farmers we support. This 20-year milestone is a testament to the resilience of Zimbabwean agriculture."

To celebrate, we are launching a series of community field days and offering specialized discounts on our top-tier fertilizers and pesticides throughout the month.
